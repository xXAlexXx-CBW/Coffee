"Coffee is an invigorating, energizing strong drink made from the fruit of the coffee tree. The first samples of this drink belong to the ancient monks who found wild berries on one of the bushes. Over time, the monks noticed that brewed coffee beans helped to eliminate drowsiness, which was attractive for night prayers."

"What is a coffee drink? This is a large number of different ingredients added to classic black coffee, which create amazing cocktails for all tastes. In the modern world, everyone can try absolutely any kind - natural espresso or aromatic latte, less strong Americano or delicious lungo in terms of aromatic composition."
